ABOUT US

Anand Agro Products, has been serving in the benefit of its customers since past 10 years. We are here to provide you easy access to products essential for living a healthy lifestyle. Anand Agro is most trusted and is popular for its turmeric powder and other turmeric products. We connect you to the purest of essential grocery products sourced from our own organic farms, which are 100% unadulterated and manufactured by safe hands. We currently home deliver fresh chemical-free Spices (Masale), Powders, Pickles, Jaggery and many other products to our customers in and around Pune, Wai and Satara city.

Anand Agro is binded to the principles & values of organic farming, those are of ‘Swasthya’(Health) , ‘Paristhitikee’(Ecology), ‘Nishpakshata’(Fairness) and ‘Dekh-bhaal’(Care)
